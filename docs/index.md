# The Randolph Glacier Inventory

The **Randolph Glacier Inventory (RGI)** is a globally complete dataset of glacier outlines. It is a contribution of the [IACS Working Group on the Randolph Glacier Inventory](https://cryosphericsciences.org/activities/working-groups/rgi-working-group/) and is drawn from the [Global Land Ice Measurements from Space (GLIMS)](https://www.glims.org) database.

The RGI excludes the ice sheets of Greenland and Antarctica and is designed as a snapshot in time, currently targeting the year 2000. It includes all glaciers larger than 0.01 km² and prioritises global coverage and consistency over precision for individual glaciers. It is best suited for regional and global assessments of glacier area, volume, and change.

RGI 7.0 was released on **19 September 2023** and is the recommended version for all new studies.
